Temporary match chat

Ratings & reputation

Calendar & reminders

How Kawwer works

From an empty calendar to a full pitch — without sending a single "who's in?" message.

STEP 01

Create the match

Pick a saved field and Kawwer fills in the price, fee and duration automatically — snapshotted so future price changes never touch this match.

STEP 02

Invite your crew

Send invites to friends and groups like "Friday Friends." Players reply in one tap; a fair waiting list handles the overflow for you.

STEP 03

Play & collect

On match day, track who arrived and collect each player's exact share. Finish with the balance at zero — every time.

Frequently asked questions

It's Tunisian for "round it up" — round up the ball, round up your friends, and get the game going. That's the whole idea: less coordinating, more playing.

Kawwer is built for cash games. Each player's share is the remaining amount divided by accepted players, rounded up, with the organizer absorbing the rounding remainder. You can record full or partial payments, split the balance, and finish only when the remaining amount is zero. Every transaction is stored in an immutable ledger.

Extra players who accept join a waiting list with strict "first accepted, first promoted" ordering. If someone leaves, the next player in line is promoted automatically — fair, and no manual juggling.

No. Live location is entirely optional and surfaced only through the Live Match screen. Sharing starts when you choose and stops when the match ends.
