# Ratings & Reputation System

## Purpose

The Ratings & Reputation System helps organizers identify reliable players while encouraging fair play and respectful behavior.

A player's reputation is calculated automatically based on match participation and community feedback.

---

# Reputation

Every user has a Reputation Score.

The score is calculated automatically.

Users cannot modify it.

---

# Reliability Badge

The application displays one badge instead of the raw score.

Possible badges:

🟢 Very Reliable

🟢 Reliable

🟡 Occasionally Cancels

🟠 Often Late

🔴 Frequent No-Show

Badges update automatically whenever reputation changes.

---

# Reputation Factors

Positive actions:

- Attends matches.
- Pays on time.
- Rarely cancels.
- Receives good organizer ratings.

Negative actions:

- Cancels within 48 hours.
- Does not attend.
- Frequently arrives late.
- Receives poor organizer ratings.
- Leaves without paying.

---

# Match Rating

After every finished match, each participant may rate:

- Organizer
- Other participants

Ratings use a five-star scale.

★★★★★

Each participant may rate another participant only once per match.

---

# Organizer Rating

Players evaluate:

- Communication
- Organization
- Accuracy of match information

---

# Player Rating

Participants evaluate:

- Sportsmanship
- Fair play
- Reliability

---

# Rating Rules

Ratings become available only after:

Match Status = Finished

Ratings close:

Seven days after match completion.

---

# Organizer View

The organizer can see:

- Average player rating
- Reliability badge
- Number of completed matches
- Number of late cancellations
- Number of no-shows

---

# Player Profile

Visible information:

- Reliability badge
- Average rating
- Matches played
- Organizer rating
- Player rating

Private calculations remain hidden.

---

# Business Rules

- Ratings are anonymous.
- One rating per player per match.
- Ratings cannot be edited after submission.
- Reputation updates automatically.
- Reputation history is retained.

---

# Acceptance Criteria

- Players can rate after finished matches.
- Duplicate ratings are prevented.
- Reputation updates automatically.
- Reliability badge changes correctly.
- Reputation cannot be manually edited.
