# Statistics

## Purpose

The Statistics system provides meaningful insights into player activity, organizer performance, attendance, and community participation.

Statistics are informational only and never modify business rules.

---

# Overview

Every user has a personal Statistics page.

Statistics are updated automatically after each completed match.

Historical statistics are never deleted.

---

# Player Statistics

## General

Displayed values:

- Matches Played
- Matches Organized
- Invitations Received
- Invitations Accepted
- Invitations Declined
- Public Matches Joined

---

## Attendance

Displayed values:

- Attendance Rate
- On-Time Arrivals
- Late Arrivals
- No-Shows
- Late Cancellations

Attendance Rate

= Matches Attended / Accepted Matches

---

## Payments

Displayed values:

- Payment Reliability
- Payments Completed
- Partial Payments
- Outstanding Payments

Payment Reliability

= Fully Paid Matches / Accepted Matches

---

## Reputation

Displayed values:

- Reliability Badge
- Average Rating
- Organizer Rating
- Player Rating

---

## Social

Displayed values:

- Friends
- Groups Created
- Favorite Organizers
- Public Matches Hosted

---

# Organizer Statistics

Displayed values:

- Matches Organized
- Matches Completed
- Matches Cancelled
- Average Match Fill Time
- Average Attendance
- Average Player Rating
- Average Organizer Rating

---

# Match Statistics

Every match displays:

- Accepted Players
- Declined Players
- Waiting List Size
- Attendance Rate
- Total Money Collected
- Collection Completion Time

---

# Monthly Statistics

Every month the application generates:

- Matches Played
- Matches Organized
- Attendance Rate
- Payment Reliability
- Reputation Changes

---

# Yearly Statistics

Every year the application generates:

- Matches Played
- Matches Organized
- Average Attendance
- Total Players Met
- Public Match Participation

---

# Trends

The application displays trends for:

- Reputation
- Attendance
- Organizer Rating
- Payment Reliability

Trend values:

- Improving
- Stable
- Declining

---

# Privacy

Users may choose whether statistics are:

- Public
- Friends Only
- Private

Reputation badges remain visible regardless of profile visibility.

---

# Business Rules

- Statistics are recalculated automatically.
- Statistics cannot be edited manually.
- Historical values are preserved.
- Deleted matches are excluded from calculations.
