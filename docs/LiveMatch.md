# Live Match

## Purpose

The Live Match screen becomes the organizer's control center once players begin travelling to the football field.

It combines attendance, live location, payments and match progress into a single interface.

The goal is to eliminate the need for WhatsApp messages and phone calls during match day.

---

# Activation

Live Match becomes available:

2 hours before kickoff.

The organizer may activate it manually.

---

# Live Location

The organizer may request live location sharing from every participant.

Each player receives a notification asking for permission.

Sharing location is optional.

Location sharing automatically stops when:

- The player disables it.
- The match finishes.
- The organizer ends Live Match.

---

# Map

The map displays:

- Football field
- Organizer
- Players sharing location

Each player marker displays:

- Name
- Distance from football field
- Estimated arrival time

---

# Attendance

Each participant has one attendance status.

Possible values:

- Travelling
- Arrived
- Late
- No Show

The organizer may manually update attendance.

Players may also mark themselves as:

"I've Arrived"

---

# Payment Summary

The Live Match screen displays:

- Total amount required
- Amount collected
- Remaining amount
- Paid players
- Unpaid players

The organizer may immediately open the Payment Dashboard.

---

# Match Summary

Displayed information:

- Match title
- Football field
- Date
- Kickoff time
- Countdown
- Accepted players
- Waiting list
- Weather (future)

---

# Needs Attention

The organizer immediately sees important events.

Examples:

- Two player spots remain.
- Three players have not responded.
- One player cancelled.
- Four players have not paid.
- One player is late.
- Match begins in thirty minutes.

---

# Organizer Actions

The organizer may:

- Open Payments
- Open Match Chat
- Send Reminder
- Request Live Location
- Mark Attendance
- Cancel Match
- View Waiting List

---

# Player Actions

Participants may:

- Share Location
- Stop Sharing
- Mark Arrived
- Open Navigation
- Open Match Chat

---

# Notifications

Automatic notifications:

- Live Match started.
- Location request.
- Match begins in thirty minutes.
- Match begins in ten minutes.
- Match cancelled.
- Waiting list promotion.

---

# Business Rules

- Location sharing is always optional.
- Only accepted participants may share location.
- Organizer always sees payment progress.
- Attendance may be edited until match completion.

---

# Acceptance Criteria

- Organizer can start Live Match.
- Players can share location.
- Players can stop sharing.
- Attendance updates correctly.
- Payment summary updates automatically.
- Needs Attention updates in real time.
