# Kawwer — Product Vision

**Status:** Approved · **Version:** 1.0 · **Last Updated:** 2026-06-30 · **Owner:** Kawwer Team

---

## The Problem

Organizing a casual football match in Tunisia is a logistical chore. An organizer juggles
WhatsApp groups, phone calls and cash to answer the same questions every week: *Who is playing?
Who confirmed? Who is on the waiting list? Who has paid? Who is actually on their way?* The
information is scattered across chat threads and memory, and the organizer carries the burden
alone.

## The Vision

**Kawwer turns the weekly football match into a single, calm workflow.** From creating a match to
collecting the last dinar at the field, every step lives in one application — invitations,
responses, a fair waiting list, match-day attendance, cash collection, and a temporary chat — so
organizers stop chasing people and players always know where they stand.

## Who It Is For

- **Organizers** who run recurring games and want confirmations, payments and reminders handled
  for them.
- **Players** who want to be invited, reply in one tap, see the field on a map, and pay their
  exact share.
- **Communities** of friends, colleagues and university teams who play together regularly.

## What Makes Kawwer Different

- **Built around the real Tunisian football night**: cash payments, trusted groups, and
  organizer-controlled public matches (decisions D-014, D-018).
- **One lifecycle, not ten screens**: a player's invitation, response, waiting-list spot,
  attendance and payment are a single coherent story.
- **A match-day control center** (Live Match) that replaces the flurry of calls and messages with
  attendance, payment progress, and optional live location in one place (D-015).
- **Trust you can see**: automatic reputation and reliability badges derived from real
  participation, never manually edited (D-017, D-022).

## Guiding Principles

1. **Reduce taps.** The common actions — invite, accept, mark paid — are one tap.
2. **Never spam.** Notifications are timely and relevant; critical ones can't be silenced.
3. **The organizer stays in control.** Especially for who joins a match.
4. **Honest data.** Statistics and reputation are computed, not entered.
5. **One source of truth.** The mobile app talks only to the backend API (D-024).

## Scope

- **Version 1** delivers the full match lifecycle for cash games. See `Roadmap.md` and
  `Features.md`.
- **Version 2** adds convenience and intelligence: templates, recurring matches, tournaments,
  leagues, AI team balancing and a richer statistics dashboard.

## Success Looks Like

- An organizer creates and fills a match without sending a single WhatsApp message.
- Players know their status and exact share at a glance.
- Cash is fully collected before kickoff, every time.
- Reliable players are visibly recognized, making pickup games fairer and friendlier.
